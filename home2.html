The Psychological Trauma and Stress Systems Lab Team

Led by Dr. R. Nicholas Carleton, a clinical psychologist and expert of psychological trauma in Canadian PSP. Our team is dedicated to helping PSP thrive. Backed by clinicians, researchers, and operational staff, we provide evidence-informed lifesaving solutions to reduce the impacts of trauma and stress.
